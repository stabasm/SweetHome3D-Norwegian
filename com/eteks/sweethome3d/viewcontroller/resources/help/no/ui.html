Sweet Home 3D Hjelp
     Sweet Home 3D Brukergrensesnitt
 

Hvert Sweet Home 3D vindu redigerer interiør-design for et hjem og er delt inn i fire ruter som kan endre størrelse. Disse er.

1.

Møbelkatalogen.
Denne katalogen er organisert i valgbare kategorier og inneholder alle møbler og objekter som du kan legge til hjem-designet ditt.

2.

Hjemmets møbelliste.
Denne listen inneholder alle møbler/objekter i hjemmet ditt hvor navnet, størrelsen og andre attributter kan vises. Listen kan sorteres ved å klikke på hver kolonne-tittel eller med Møbler > Sorter etter menyen, og hvilke kolonner som vises kan endres med Møbler > Vis kolonne menyen.

3.

The home plan.
This pane displays your home as seen from top. It is the place where you draw the walls and the rooms of your home and where you layout your furniture with the mouse or the keyboard arrows. When a home has more than one level, the name of each level is displayed in tabs at the top of the plan.

4.

The home 3D view.
This pane displays your home in 3 dimensions. You may see your home in this pane either from the top, or from a virtual visitor point of view.

Each of these panes may have the focus, and some operations proposed by Sweet Home 3D menus depend on the focused pane, recognizable by the colored rectangle that surrounds it. To transfer focus to another pane, use tab and shift + tab keys, or click in the pane that should gain focus.

According to your system, the tools bar on the top of these four panes may be moved out the window or placed on one of the three other borders of the window by dragging and dropping it with the mouse. The 3D view can be detached from its window by choosing 3D view > Display in separate window menu item.

User preferences and other menu items let you customize a Sweet Home 3D window in many ways. As the following image of the same home shows, the language and the unit used in Sweet Home 3D may be changed, the furniture catalog may be displayed as a searchable list, the columns in the furniture list may be modified, objects in the plan may be viewed in a different way, the navigation arrows in the 3D view may be hidden...
